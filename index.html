Built for RGP, Ortho-K & scleral lens wearers · Pre-launch

Hard lenses deserve better than fingertips.

RGP, Ortho-K, and scleral lenses are unforgiving — they resist placement, and a single drop can mean breakage and a $1,000–$2,000 replacement. Most aids on the market are made for soft lenses and still leave you holding your own eyelids open. Insertact is purpose-built for high-stakes lenses: it stabilizes the eyelid and guides the lens to the cornea in one motion.

How it works

Three controlled steps.

  1. 01

    Stabilize the lids

    Integrated eyelid stabilization holds the eye open for you — no free hand required, even for kids or limited dexterity.

  2. 02

    Place with precision

    A precision suction mechanism secures the lens and guides it onto the cornea, overcoming the eye's natural reflexes.

  3. 03

    Remove the same way

    The same controlled motion lifts the lens back out at any angle — one device for the full routine, finger-free.
